About me

I am currently an Assistant Professor at the School of Mathematics and Statistics, Shenzhen University. I received my Ph.D. degree in Mathematics from Sun Yat-sen University in 2022, under the supervision of Professor Chengxi Wang. Before that, I received my B.Sc. degree in Mathematics from Hunan Normal University in 2017.

My research interests include nonlinear partial differential equations, especially reaction-diffusion equations, chemotaxis models, and their applications in biology and other fields. I focus on the analysis of the long-term behavior and pattern formation in complex dynamical systems governed by PDEs.
