About Me

I am currently an Assistant Professor at the School of Mathematics and Statistics, Shenzhen University. I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics from Sun Yat-sen University in 2022, under the supervision of Professor Chengxi Wang. Prior to that, I earned my bachelor's degree from Hunan Normal University in 2017.

My research interests include nonlinear partial differential equations, especially reaction-diffusion equations, chemotaxis models, and their applications in biology and other fields. I focus on the analysis of the long-term behavior and pattern formation in complex dynamical systems governed by PDEs.
