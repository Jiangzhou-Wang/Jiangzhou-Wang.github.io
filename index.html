About me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at the School of Mathematical Sciences, Shenzhen University. I received my Ph.D. in Statistics from Northeast Normal University under the supervision of Professor Jianhua Guo. Subsequently, I completed postdoctoral research at the Southern University of Science and Technology, working with Professors Bingyi Jing and Qiman Shao.

My research primarily focuses on the statistical analysis of large-scale network data, multiple testing in high-dimensional and dependent settings, and the interface between statistics, machine learning, and deep learning. I have published in leading statistical journals, including JASA, JCGS, JMVA, CSDA, Computational Statistics, Stat, and others.

Some postdoctoral positions are available now. Please feel free to contact me with your CV if you are interested.
