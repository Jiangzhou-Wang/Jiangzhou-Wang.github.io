About me

Hi! I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at the School of Mathematical Sciences, Shenzhen University. I received my Ph.D. degree in Statistics from Northeast Normal University under the supervision of Professor Jianhua Guo. Later, I conducted postdoctoral research at Southern University of Science and Technology, collaborating with Professors Bingyi Jing and Qiman Shao.

My primary research interests focus on statistical analysis of large-scale network data, multiple testing for large-scale dependent data, and the intersection of statistics with machine learning and deep learning. I have published papers in leading statistical journals such as JASA, JCGS, JMVA, CSDA, Computational Statistics, and Stat ect.

There are some openings for postdocs. Please feel free to contact me with your CV if you are interested..
